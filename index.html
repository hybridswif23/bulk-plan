⚙️ Sunday Prep Guide (~75 min)
1

Preheat Oven 200°C

Season ALL 2.8kg chicken thighs with olive oil, salt, garlic powder, paprika. Lay on 2–3 trays.

2

Bake Chicken (25–30 min)

All at once. Internal temp 75°C. Cool fully before boxing — prevents sogginess.

3

Cook Pasta in Bulk

Boil 560g dry pasta once. Drain, toss with olive oil to stop sticking. Split into 7 containers.

4

🧠 Appetite Tips
⏰

Eat by the clock, not hunger

Set 6 alarms. After 2–3 weeks your body demands food at those times.

💧

No water before/during meals

Drink between meals only. Adds 200–400 kcal of stomach capacity.

🏋️

Train before biggest meal

Exercise spikes hunger hormones 60–90 min post-workout.

🚶

10-min walk after meals

Speeds digestion, makes you hungry faster for next meal.

⚡ 7 Non-Negotiable Rules
1

Never skip a meal — missing 1 = 600+ kcal gone. No catch-up.

2

Weigh every Monday morning — same time, after toilet, before eating.

3

Weight not moving 5+ days → add 200–300 kcal (extra glass of milk or snack).

4

Hit your protein every single day — everything else can vary, protein cannot.

5

Rest days: eat the SAME amount. Muscles grow on rest days, not gym days.

6

Every workout: add reps or weight vs last session. No progression = no muscle.

7

Sleep 7–9 hours. No sleep = no growth hormone = wasted calories + wasted reps.
