Appetite Tips

Eat by the clock, not hunger

Set 6 alarms. After 2–3 weeks your body demands food at those times.

No water before or during meals

Drink between meals only. Adds 200–400 kcal of stomach capacity.

Train before your biggest meal

Exercise spikes hunger hormones for 60–90 min post-workout.

10-min walk after meals

Speeds digestion. Hungry faster for the next meal.

Non-Negotiable Rules
1

Never skip a meal. Missing one = 600+ kcal gone. No catch-up.

2

Weigh every Monday morning. Same time, after toilet, before eating.

3

Weight not moving 5+ days → add 200–300 kcal.

4

Hit protein every single day. Everything else can vary, protein cannot.

5

Rest days: eat the same amount. Muscles grow on rest days.

6

Every workout: add reps or weight vs last session.

7

Sleep 7–9 hours. No sleep = no growth hormone.
